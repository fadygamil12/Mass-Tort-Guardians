Roundup

Roundup, the most popular herbicide in the world, is currently a focal point in numerous legal proceedings owing to its key component, glyphosate, which has been associated with cancer risk. The International Agency for Research on Cancer has labeled glyphosate as potentially carcinogenic to humans, leading many Roundup users to pursue legal remedies.
