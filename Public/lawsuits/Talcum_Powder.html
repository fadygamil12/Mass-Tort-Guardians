Talcum Powder

Recent product liability litigation has brought talcum powder lawsuits to the forefront, aiming to hold manufacturers accountable for purportedly neglecting to communicate health risks linked to talc products. These lawsuits, spanning all 50 states, claim a correlation between talc-based products and different types of cancer.
